DATA ROWS (Copy one row at a time):
===============================================

UC001 | User Registration | Allows patients to create an account by providing name, email, phone number, and password. System validates email format and password strength (minimum 8 characters).

UC002 | User Login | Enables patients to log into the system using registered email and password. System validates credentials against database.

UC003 | Password Reset | Allows users to reset forgotten passwords via email link with secure JWT token that expires in 15 minutes.

UC004 | Hospital/Staff Login | Enables hospital staff and dermatologists to access the facility dashboard using authenticated credentials.

UC005 | Logout | Allows users and hospital staff to securely log out, clearing session tokens and protecting account security.

UC006 | Create Health Profile | Patients can create health profiles with medical history, current medications, known allergies, and previous skin conditions.

UC007 | Update Health Profile | Allows patients to modify existing health information, add new medications, or update allergy information.

UC008 | View Health Profile | Displays complete health profile including medical history, risk factors, and previous analysis results.

UC009 | Upload Skin Image | Patients can upload clear photos of skin lesions from device gallery for AI analysis.

UC010 | Capture Skin Image | Allows real-time image capture using device camera with guidelines for proper lighting and distance.

UC011 | Validate Image Content | System automatically checks images for inappropriate content (NSFW filter) and verifies minimum 25% skin content before analysis.

UC012 | Reject Non-Skin Images | System rejects posters, text documents, or images without sufficient skin content with clear error messages.

UC013 | Complete Symptom Questionnaire | Patients answer guided questions about symptoms: duration, pain level, itching, bleeding, size changes, and location.

UC014 | AI Lesion Analysis | Vision Transformer model analyzes validated images, detects lesion location, classifies into 7 cancer types with confidence scores.

UC015 | Display AI Results | Shows analysis results with cancer type classification, confidence percentage, risk level (low/medium/high/urgent), and recommended actions.

UC016 | Generate Risk Assessment | AI system assigns risk levels based on cancer type and confidence: Melanoma/High confidence = Urgent, others classified accordingly.

UC017 | Save Analysis Report | System stores complete analysis including image, AI predictions, symptom responses, and risk assessment in patient history.

UC018 | View Analysis History | Patients can access all previous skin analyses with dates, results, and risk levels in chronological order.

UC019 | Search Nearby Hospitals | Uses patient's GPS location or manual address to find dermatology hospitals and general hospitals within 50km radius via Google Maps API.

UC020 | Filter Hospital Results | Displays up to 180 hospitals sorted by rating (4.5+ first) then distance, showing ratings, distance, and operating hours.

UC021 | View Hospital Details | Shows comprehensive facility information: specialties, contact details, address, operating hours, and patient reviews.

UC022 | Get Navigation Directions | Provides turn-by-turn directions from patient's location to selected hospital using Google Maps integration.

UC023 | Book Appointment | Patients select hospital, preferred date/time, attach analysis report, and submit appointment request with urgency level.

UC024 | View Reports by Urgency | Hospital staff see incoming patient reports organized by risk level (Urgent → High → Medium → Low) for clinical prioritization.

UC025 | Review Patient Report | Staff can view AI analysis results, skin images, symptom questionnaire, but patient identity remains hidden until appointment confirmed.

UC026 | Access Patient History | After confirming appointment, staff can access patient's complete medical history, previous analyses, and health profile for consultation preparation.

UC027 | Confirm Appointment | Hospital staff accept appointment requests, select available time slot, and system sends confirmation email to patient automatically.

UC028 | Reject Appointment | Staff can decline appointments with reason (fully booked, requires different specialist, etc.) and system notifies patient via email.
