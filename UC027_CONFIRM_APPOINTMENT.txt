Table 4.29   Use Case Description for <UC027 Confirm Appointment>
================================================================================

Use Case ID         | UC027

Use Case Name       | Confirm Appointment

Actors              | Hospital/Clinic Staff, Email System (SMTP), Patient (receives)

Description         | How hospital staff accepts and schedules appointment requests
                    | by selecting date/time, setting consultation type (in-person
                    | or video), and revealing patient identity after confirmation.
                    | System sends confirmation email with appointment details and
                    | grants staff access to full patient medical history for
                    | consultation preparation.

Pre-conditions      | 1. Staff has reviewed patient report (UC025)
                    | 2. Staff decides to accept appointment request
                    | 3. Appointment status is currently "Pending"
                    | 4. Hospital has available slots for consultation
                    | 5. Patient identity is currently HIDDEN from staff

Post-conditions     | 1. Appointment status changes to "Confirmed"
                    | 2. Patient identity is REVEALED to staff
                    | 3. Staff gains access to patient full medical history
                    | 4. Patient receives confirmation email with date/time
                    | 5. Appointment added to hospital calendar
                    | 6. Appointment removed from pending queue
                    | 7. Appointment appears in staff's confirmed appointments list

Normal Flow (NF)    | 1. Staff reviews report (UC025), clicks "Confirm Appointment"
                    | 2. System displays confirmation form; staff selects date from
                    |    hospital calendar, then selects time slot (30-min intervals)
                    | 3. Staff chooses consultation type (in-person/video), estimates
                    |    duration (15-60 min based on risk), adds optional instructions
                    | 4. Staff reviews details and clicks "Confirm and Notify Patient"
                    | 5. System updates appointment status to "Confirmed", REVEALS
                    |    patient identity to staff, grants access to medical history,
                    |    adds to calendar, logs privacy access for HIPAA/GDPR compliance
                    | 6. System sends confirmation email to patient with appointment
                    |    details, hospital location/directions, preparation instructions,
                    |    and calendar attachment links
                    | 7. Staff dashboard updates: pending count decreases, confirmed
                    |    count increases, patient name now visible, staff can access
                    |    full medical history for consultation preparation

Alternative Flow    | AF1-UC027: Video Consultation Setup
(AF)                | Staff selects "Video Consultation"; system generates unique video
                    | room URL, includes link in confirmation email (activates 15 min
                    | before), sends SMS reminder, and provides separate host link to staff
                    |
                    | AF2-UC027: Urgent Case Priority Scheduling
                    | For "URGENT" risk level reports, system highlights earliest slots
                    | within 48 hours, allows schedule override, marks email as HIGH
                    | PRIORITY, schedules multiple reminders, shows URGENT badge on dashboard
                    |
                    | AF3-UC027: Reschedule Existing Slot
                    | If selected time is already booked, system displays "Time slot
                    | unavailable" warning and suggests next 5 available slots for selection
                    |
                    | AF4-UC027: Request Additional Patient Information
                    | Staff clicks "Request Information" before confirmation; system sends
                    | anonymous email to patient; patient responds via secure portal; staff
                    | receives notification and proceeds with confirmation after reviewing response

Exception Flow (EF) | EF1: No Available Slots in Next 30 Days
                    | System displays "No availability" warning and suggests extending
                    | search to 60 days or recommending alternative hospital; for urgent
                    | cases, staff must manually add extra slot or reject with referral
                    |
                    | EF2: Email Send Failed
                    | SMTP error occurs; appointment still confirmed in database; system
                    | logs error, queues retry, displays warning to staff to contact
                    | patient by phone; patient can see confirmation in account dashboard
                    |
                    | EF3: Patient Has No Email Address
                    | System displays warning before confirmation; staff must obtain valid
                    | email/phone, contact patient directly, manually add to record
                    |
                    | EF4: Consultation Type Not Selected
                    | "Please select consultation type" message appears; submit button
                    | remains disabled until selection made (required for patient preparation)
                    |
                    | EF5: Calendar Sync Failed
                    | External calendar integration error; appointment still saved in
                    | SkinGuard database; system displays sync failure warning; staff can
                    | manually add to external calendar; confirmation email still sent
                    |
                    | EF6: Privacy Access Logging Failed
                    | Audit log write error; system blocks identity reveal until logging
                    | succeeds; displays "Cannot reveal patient data - compliance logging
                    | failed" warning; staff must retry to ensure HIPAA/GDPR compliance

================================================================================
WORKFLOW SUMMARY
================================================================================

STEPS OVERVIEW:
1. Staff clicks "Confirm Appointment" (Decision made after UC025 review)
2. Select date/time from calendar (Steps 4-8)
3. Choose consultation type and duration (Steps 9-11)
4. Add optional instructions (Steps 12-14)
5. Review and submit confirmation (Steps 17-18)
6. System processes and REVEALS patient identity (Steps 19-21)
7. System adds to calendar and logs access (Steps 22-23)
8. System sends confirmation email (Step 24)
9. Dashboard updates and staff gains full access (Steps 25-28)

PRIVACY PROTECTION MECHANISM:
- Before confirmation: Patient identity HIDDEN (anonymized review)
- During confirmation: Identity remains hidden until final submit
- After confirmation: Identity REVEALED + access granted + audit logged
- For rejections (UC028): Identity NEVER revealed (stays anonymous)

KEY SYSTEM BEHAVIORS:
✅ Two-phase process: Review anonymous → Confirm and reveal
✅ Audit logging before identity reveal (compliance)
✅ Automatic calendar blocking (prevents double-booking)
✅ Risk-based scheduling suggestions (urgent cases prioritized)
✅ Multiple reminder frequencies by priority level
✅ Staff gains access to full medical history post-confirmation

COMPLIANCE NOTES:
- HIPAA: Patient identity access logged in audit trail
- GDPR: Explicit purpose for data access (appointment confirmation)
- Privacy-by-design: Identity hidden until commitment made
- Audit trail: Staff ID, timestamp, reason logged
