Vision Transformer AI System (ViT) | An advanced deep learning model that validates uploaded images for skin content, detects and localizes skin lesions, classifies them into 7 cancer types (Melanoma, Basal Cell Carcinoma, Actinic Keratoses, Benign Keratosis, Dermatofibroma, Vascular Lesions, Melanocytic Nevi) with 84% accuracy, and assesses risk levels (low, medium, high, urgent) for clinical prioritization.

Google Maps API | External mapping service that uses the patient's GPS location or manual address input to search for dermatology hospitals and general hospitals within a 50km radius, displays hospital ratings and distances, calculates navigation routes, and provides up to 180 nearby medical facilities sorted by rating and proximity.

Email System (SMTP) | Automated notification service that sends appointment confirmation emails when hospitals accept patient requests, appointment cancellation notifications with reasons, password reset links with secure JWT tokens for account recovery, and other system notifications to patients and hospital staff.
