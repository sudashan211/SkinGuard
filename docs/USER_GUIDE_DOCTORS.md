## Getting Started

### What is SkinGuard?

SkinGuard is an AI-powered skin cancer screening platform that:
- Provides patients with preliminary risk assessments
- Connects patients with verified dermatologists
- Facilitates remote and in-person consultations
- Streamlines the diagnostic workflow

### Your Role

As a verified doctor on SkinGuard, you:
- Review AI-analyzed patient reports
- Provide professional medical consultations
- Add clinical notes and recommendations
- Help patients understand their results
- Recommend further testing when needed

### Platform Benefits

- **Pre-screened Patients:** AI filters and prioritizes cases
- **Complete Information:** Access to images, symptoms, and patient history
- **Flexible Consultations:** In-person or video options
- **Efficient Workflow:** Organized dashboard with urgent case prioritization
- **Patient Reach:** Connect with patients in your area

## Registration and Verification

### Step 1: Create Account

1. Visit https://skinguard.com
2. Click "Sign Up"
3. Enter your information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Password
4. Select "Doctor" as your role
5. Click "Create Account"

### Step 2: Complete Doctor Profile

After creating your account, complete your professional profile:

**Required Information:**
- **Medical License Number:** Your state medical license
- **Clinic Name:** Your practice or clinic name
- **Clinic Location:** Full address
- **Coordinates:** Auto-filled from address
- **WhatsApp Number:** For patient communication (format: +1234567890)
- **Specialization:** Dermatology, Oncology, etc.

**Optional Information:**
- **Bio:** Brief professional background
- **Education:** Medical school and training
- **Certifications:** Board certifications
- **Languages:** Languages you speak
- **Clinic Hours:** Your availability

Click "Submit for Verification"

### Step 3: Verification Process

**What Happens Next:**
1. Admin reviews your application
2. License verification with state medical board
3. Clinic information verification
4. Typically takes 1-3 business days

**Verification Status:**
- **Pending:** Under review
- **Verified:** Approved, full access granted
- **Rejected:** Contact support for details

**During Verification:**
- You can log in and explore the platform
- Cannot access patient reports
- Cannot receive appointments
- Profile not visible to patients

**After Verification:**
- Full access to patient reports
- Profile visible on doctor locator
- Can receive appointment bookings
- Receive email confirmation
