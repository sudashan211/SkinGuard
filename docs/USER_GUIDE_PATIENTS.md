## Getting Started

SkinGuard is an AI-powered platform that helps you:
- Screen skin lesions for potential cancer
- Get probability-based risk assessments
- Find verified dermatologists near you
- Book appointments and consultations
- Track your skin health over time

**Important:** SkinGuard provides screening estimates, not diagnoses. Always consult a medical professional for definitive diagnosis.

## Setting Up Your Health Profile

Complete your health profile for more accurate AI assessments.

### Navigate to Profile

1. Click your avatar in the top right
2. Select "Health Profile"

### Enter Your Information

**Age:**
- Enter your current age
- This helps assess age-related risk factors

**Skin Type (Fitzpatrick Scale):**
- Type I: Very fair, always burns, never tans
- Type II: Fair, usually burns, tans minimally
- Type III: Medium, sometimes burns, tans uniformly
- Type IV: Olive, rarely burns, tans easily
- Type V: Brown, very rarely burns, tans very easily
- Type VI: Dark brown/black, never burns, tans very easily

**Family History:**
- Note any family members with skin cancer
- Include type of cancer and age at diagnosis
- Example: "Mother had melanoma at age 60"

Click "Save Profile" when complete.

## Uploading Images for Analysis

### Taking Good Photos

For accurate analysis, follow these guidelines:

**Lighting:**
- Use natural daylight when possible
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Ensure the lesion is well-lit

**Distance:**
- Hold camera 6-12 inches from lesion
- Fill most of the frame with the lesion
- Include some surrounding skin for context

**Focus:**
- Ensure the image is sharp and clear
- Avoid blurry photos
- Take multiple shots if needed

**What to Avoid:**
- Flash photography (causes glare)
- Extreme close-ups (too blurry)
- Dark or poorly lit images
- Images with clothing or jewelry in frame

### Upload Process

#### On Desktop:

1. Go to "Diagnostic Dashboard"
2. Click "Upload Image" or drag and drop
3. Select your image file
4. Preview the image
5. Click "Continue to Symptoms"

#### On Mobile:

1. Go to "Diagnostic Dashboard"
2. Tap "Take Photo" or "Choose from Gallery"
3. Capture or select image
4. Preview and confirm
5. Tap "Continue to Symptoms"

### Symptom Wizard

Complete the 3-step symptom wizard:

**Step 1: Location**
- Select where the lesion is located on your body
- Use the body map to pinpoint the area

**Step 2: Sensations**
- Check any sensations you're experiencing:
  - Itching
  - Pain
  - Burning
  - Numbness
  - None

**Step 3: Visual Changes**
- Check any changes you've noticed:
  - Color change
  - Size increase
  - Shape change
  - Border irregularity
  - None

**Duration:**
- How long have you noticed this lesion?
- Example: "2 weeks", "3 months", "1 year"

Click "Submit for Analysis"

### Analysis Time

- Analysis typically takes 5-10 seconds
- You'll see a progress indicator
- Don't close the browser during analysis

## Understanding Your Results

### Risk Levels

**Low Risk (Green):**
- Probability < 30%
- Monitor the lesion
- Schedule routine check-up

**Medium Risk (Yellow):**
- Probability 30-70%
- Consult a dermatologist soon
- Consider booking appointment

**High Risk (Orange):**
- Probability 70-85%
- See a dermatologist promptly
- Book appointment within 1-2 weeks

**Urgent (Red):**
- Probability > 85%
- Seek immediate medical attention
- Emergency consultation recommended
- Nearest doctors automatically notified

### AI Predictions

You'll see probabilities for 7 cancer types:

1. **Melanoma:** Most serious, can spread quickly
2. **Basal Cell Carcinoma:** Most common, slow-growing
3. **Squamous Cell Carcinoma:** Second most common
4. **Actinic Keratosis:** Pre-cancerous lesion
5. **Benign Keratosis:** Non-cancerous growth
6. **Dermatofibroma:** Harmless skin growth
7. **Vascular Lesion:** Blood vessel growth

### Hotspot Overlay

- Red boxes show where AI detected lesions
- Multiple boxes may appear for complex lesions
- Confidence score shows AI certainty

### Important Disclaimer

**"This is a 94% probability estimate. Please consult verified doctors for clinical biopsy."**

- AI provides screening, not diagnosis
- Only a doctor can provide definitive diagnosis
- Biopsy may be needed for confirmation
- Don't delay seeing a doctor based on low probability

## Booking Appointments

### Booking Process

1. Select a doctor from the map
2. Click "Book Appointment"
3. Choose consultation type:
   - **In-Person:** Visit clinic
   - **Video:** Online consultation
4. Select date and time
5. Choose which report to discuss (optional)
6. Add any notes for the doctor
7. Click "Confirm Booking"

### Appointment Types

**In-Person Consultation:**
- Visit doctor's clinic
- Physical examination
- May include biopsy
- Bring your SkinGuard report

**Video Consultation:**
- Online video call
- Review images and AI results
- Get initial assessment
- Doctor may recommend in-person follow-up

### Appointment Confirmation

You'll receive:
- Email confirmation
- In-app notification
- Calendar invite
- Reminder 24 hours before

### Managing Appointments

## Video Consultations

- Quiet, well-lit location

### Self-Examination Guide

Monthly skin checks:

1. Use the body map guide
2. Check all areas systematically
3. Look for ABCDE signs:
   - **A**symmetry
   - **B**order irregularity
   - **C**olor variation
   - **D**iameter > 6mm
   - **E**volving (changing)

### Prevention Tips

Protect your skin:

- Use SPF 30+ sunscreen daily
- Reapply every 2 hours
- Seek shade 10am-4pm
- Wear protective clothing
- Avoid tanning beds
- Check skin monthly
- See dermatologist annually

## Troubleshooting

### Image Upload Issues

**"Image resolution too low"**
- Take a clearer photo
- Ensure good lighting
- Hold camera steady
- Minimum 512x512 pixels required

**"Inappropriate content detected"**
- Ensure image shows only skin lesion
- Remove any inappropriate content
- Contact support if error persists

**Upload fails or times out**
- Check internet connection
- Try smaller image file
- Use different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Analysis Issues

**Analysis taking too long**
- Normal time: 5-10 seconds
- If > 30 seconds, refresh page
- Check internet connection
- Try uploading again

**Can't see results**
- Refresh the page
- Check "My Reports" section
- Clear browser cache
- Contact support

### Account Issues

**Can't log in**
- Check email and password
- Use "Forgot Password" link
- Check email for reset link
- Ensure email is verified

**Not receiving emails**
- Check spam folder
- Add noreply@skinguard.com to contacts
- Verify email address in settings
- Contact support

### Video Call Issues

**Camera not working**
- Allow camera permissions
- Check browser settings
- Try different browser
- Restart device

**Poor video quality**
- Check internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps)
- Close other applications
- Move closer to router
- Use wired connection if possible
